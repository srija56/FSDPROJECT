Websocket

/ˈweb.sɒk.ɪt/

Definition

Websocket (n.): A protocol for real-time, bidirectional communication.

Example: "The chat app used WebSockets for instant updates."

Usage in FSD

  • Socket.io implements WebSocket fallbacks.
  • Stock tickers use WebSockets for live data.
